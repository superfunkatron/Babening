HOW TO USE THE BABENING
🚀 QUICK START

THE BABENING is your personal gym tracker. Three training days, your exercises, your weights — all saved to the cloud.

  1. Pick your training day (Chest, Legs, Arms)
  2. Log your weights and reps as you go
  3. Tick each exercise when done
  4. Hit the check circle on the last exercise to log the session
✏️ EDITING YOUR ROUTINE

Tap EDIT in the top right to enter Edit Mode. From there you can:

  • Add, remove and reorder exercises
  • Rename your training days
  • Add notes and how-to links to each exercise
  • Link exercises to the library for swap suggestions
📍 VENUES

In Edit Mode, tap the Venues tab to add your gyms. Once added, select your gym at the top of each training day before you start. It gets logged with your session history.

⇄ SWAP SUGGESTIONS

At a different gym and missing your usual machine? Tap ⇄ FIND SWAPS on any exercise card to see similar alternatives from the exercise library, ranked by how closely they match your usual movement.

First, link the exercise to the library in Edit Mode using the "Link to exercise library" button on each card.

➕ OTHER ACTIVITY

Log walks, rides, sport, classes and stretching sessions in the OTHER tab. These appear in your history alongside your gym sessions.

📋 HISTORY & PRs

Every completed session is saved in the LOG tab. Personal records are detected automatically and marked with 🏆. The app will also nudge you when it's time to increase your weight.
